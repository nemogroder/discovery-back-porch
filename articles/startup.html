Startup guide for turning on all the Back Porch equipment

Overview

This checklist will bring the Back Porch to a fully functioning equipment status. Follow these steps exactly to ensure everything gets connected properly. See the Setup checklist for service preparation procedures.

Step 1

Press the blinking green power light on the soundboard. It will begin it's own bootup process, so feel free to move on until it finishes.

Step 2

Wake or power on the Mac Mini

  • Mac is mounted on the back wall. The power button is on the top right.
  • Turn on the wireless keyboard and mouse
  • When it wakes up, unlock the Mac. You may have to press a few keys to wake the keyboard up
Uncover the keyboard if you want!

Step 3

Turn on the power conditioner in the rack under the desk.

The button is labeled "<-This one!"

Step 4

Turn on the three audience screens and one stage screen

  • The audeince screens are up front and use the roku and TCL remotes
  • The stage screen is behing the Porch and uses the Samsung remote

Step 5

Open and unlock the Macbook Pro lighting laptop

Step 6

Launch all required software on the two Macs

  • ProPresenter (Mac Mini)
  • Lightkey (Macbook)
  • Apple Music (Mac Mini)
  • Audio Midi Setup (Both)

Step 7

Turn on the camera by sliding the switch on top forward
