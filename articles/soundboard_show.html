A quick checklist for recalling a previously saved show on the SI Expression 3

Overview

Several shows have been saved on the soundboard to make setting up for each worship team easier. Follow these steps to load a show for a Sunday service or other event.

Step 1

On the soundboard touchscreen, press "Show"

Step 2

At the bottom of the screen, press "Load"

Step 3

Use the scroll knob to navigate and click through the file directory

  • Various special event shows are under the main MMC folder
  • Week specific Sunday shows are saved in MMC > DC Scenes

Step 4

Scroll to the show you want to load, and press "OK"

Step 5

Wait for the soundboard to finish importing the show, then proceed with the rest of your setup
